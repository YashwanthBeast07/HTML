Chemistry Notes

1. Atomic Structure

Atoms consist of protons, neutrons, and electrons.

2. Chemical Bonding

Covalent, ionic, and metallic bonds hold atoms together.

3. Organic Chemistry

Organic chemistry studies carbon-based compounds.

4. Chemical Reactions

Reactions include synthesis, decomposition, and combustion.

5. Thermodynamics

Chemical thermodynamics examines energy changes in reactions.

6. Electrochemistry

Electrochemistry deals with chemical processes that involve electricity.
