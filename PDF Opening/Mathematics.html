Mathematics Notes

Algebra

Algebra involves symbols and rules for manipulating these symbols to solve equations.

Calculus

Calculus studies rates of change (differentiation) and accumulation of quantities (integration).

Linear Algebra

Deals with vector spaces, matrices, and systems of linear equations.

Probability

Analyzes the likelihood of different outcomes in uncertain situations.

Statistics

Involves data collection, analysis, interpretation, and presentation.

Geometry

Studies shapes, sizes, and properties of space.

Trigonometry

Deals with relationships between angles and sides in triangles.

Number Theory

Focuses on properties and relationships of numbers, especially integers.

Complex Numbers

Involves numbers in the form a + bi, where i is the imaginary unit.

Graph Theory

Studies graphs and networks, used in computer science and engineering.
