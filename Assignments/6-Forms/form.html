The nonhybrid Urus models are powered by a 657-hp twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8, which burbles deeply when idling and absolutely howls under full throttle. Permanent all-wheel drive and an eight-speed automatic transmission.

Mercedes G-Wagon

The Mercedes G-Wagon is one of the most recognizable luxury SUVs in the world. Its boxy, rugged design exudes strength, elegance, and sophistication. When you drive a G-Wagon in Mumbai, you are sure to turn heads wherever you go.

BMW X-7

The BMW X7 is the German carmaker's flagship luxury SUV, available in both 6- and 7-seater layouts. It boasts a tech-loaded cabin, powerful 3-litre petrol and diesel engine options that are perfectly matched for a big SUV like this, along with BMW's signature handling.
